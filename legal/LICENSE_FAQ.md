# RepOS Licensing FAQ

## Is RepOS open-source?

Not yet.  
It is licensed under the **Business Source License 1.1**, which automatically becomes **Apache 2.0 on 2029-12-01**.

---

## Can I use RepOS for work?

Yes — as long as it is not used in production or as part of a commercial offering.

“Internal developer tooling” is allowed.

---

## Can I build a SaaS on RepOS?

Not without a **commercial license** from TriFactoria.

---

## Can I fork the project?

Yes — but you cannot use your fork commercially before the Change Date.

---

## Why BSL and not a traditional open-source license?

So that:
- Developers can freely use and contribute  
- Businesses cannot steal the work and monetize it before TriFactoria is ready  
- The project becomes open-source in the future

---

## Will RepOS be open-source later?

Yes — the license automatically converts to **Apache 2.0 on 2029-12-01**.

---

## Do contributors need to sign anything?

Yes.  
All contributors must sign the **ICLA** (and CCLA if contributing on behalf of a company).
